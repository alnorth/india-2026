Our second day of cycling was a bit harder than the first. The roads we were on were a but busier, and I was feeling a bit off for the last 20 miles or so.

Leaving Pondicherry took a while. We tried to leave a bit early, but the traffic was already building to rush hour. Threading through the other vehicles is fun challenge at times, and bit nervewracking at others.

A lot of the roads today felt quite busy, without the relief of the quiet village backroads that we'd enjoyed yesterday.

About 40 miles in I started to feel a bit nauseous, so we had to stop for a bit. After a rest by the side of the road we pushed on to the next town and I had a bottle of Coke, which helped a lot. We carried on the rest of the ride at a gentler pace, and I had a good lie down when I got to the hotel.
